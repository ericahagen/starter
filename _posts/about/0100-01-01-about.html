The Map Kibera Trust is a Kenyan registered Trust, the organizational home of our work, founded in 2010. We're working for a world in which all people have equal ability to create and share information to influence their future, by contributing to a culture where digital story-telling, open data and geographic information lead to greater influence and representation for marginalized communities, especially in Kibera, Nairobi, and Kenya.

Learn more about our people and the groups that support us on these pages.
